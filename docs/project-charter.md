# Project Charter

## Name
Global CyberGuard Intelligence Hub

## Track
Reasoning Agents

## Required IQ
Foundry IQ

## Problem
Complex cases are slow and difficult to review when evidence is fragmented across documents, logs, reports, and policies.

## Solution
A multi-agent system that analyzes synthetic evidence, builds a timeline, detects contradictions, evaluates compliance and risk, and generates a final report.

## Users
Analysts, compliance teams, security teams, risk teams

## Inputs
Synthetic documents, logs, reports, and policies

## Outputs
Findings, timeline, contradictions, compliance observations, risk score, final recommendation
